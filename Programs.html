Teaching Methodologies

COBASS H.S.S has adopted an innovative and advanced scientific teaching-learning methodology in an exclusive way. The teaching is entirely interactive and student-centered. The lectures are supported by student's full participation in their studies, group discussion, project assignment and field-trips. Apart from that, audio-visual aid, multimedia projector are used to mark teaching and learning in a more interesting, effective and purposeful manner.

Science Strem

21st century is the age of science and technology. Science Strem is a combination of subjects to educated science and Technology for the students with a passion for inquiry. Hence, science graduates can garb many opportunities in the current world. Thus, we emphasize Science education. COBASS H.S.S has separate, well-eqippped laboratories for physics, chemistry, biology, and computer science. Higher Secondary education in Science entities students the eligibility to pursue a career in MBBS, Engineering, Biotechnology, and other applied and theoretical disciplines of Science.

Management Strem

Students in the management stream are taught and trained with managerial skills and knowledge required at present. Management studies help students to develop a successful professional career in the corporate world of competition, globalization, and evolving new technology. Moreover, it helps in further studies like BBA, BCA, BIT, ACCA, CA, Industrial Management, and Human Resource Management.

Philophy & Vision

We believe education is the best wealth among all kinds and it can apply to every student. We want our students to inculcate a positive attitude towards learning and innovating new ideas with a keen sense of critical thinking. COBASS H.S.S adopts the philosophy of unifying the physical, emotional excellence in every individual and transform into social scientific character. We assist our students in setting up realistic goals and support them in undertaking challenges to release their potentialities.

The Further Vision

  • Nurturing the inherent potentials of each student providing a flexible, innovative, and challenging environment.
  • To encourage students to become both independent and unified in teamwork through learning and sharing.
  • To conduct researches, seminars, and workshops to develop the outstanding talent of our students and faculties.
  • To prepare students thoroughly for the university and professional level like Science & Technology, Engineering, MBBS, Nursing and BBA, BIT, CA, BSCIT.
